BrainSpark Expands First-Party Data Partnerships

BS
BrainSpark Team May 28, 20264 min read

BrainSpark is expanding its first-party data partnerships, bringing even richer shopper signal into Spark DSP — and helping advertisers reach the right audience at exactly the right moment, without relying on third-party cookies.

Stronger signal, fewer guesses

First-party data from our owned-and-operated apps — Weather Mint, Rewards Rabbit, and Battery Booster — already powers targeting inside Spark DSP. These new partnerships layer in additional verified shopper signal, sharpening audience precision across every connected retail network.

Built for a cookie-less future

As third-party tracking continues to erode, first-party data partnerships like these are becoming the backbone of effective retail media. BrainSpark is positioning its DSP to stay accurate and compliant as the ad ecosystem shifts.

"The future of retail media isn't more data — it's the right data, owned and verified."

— BrainSpark Data Partnerships Lead

What it means for advertisers

More signal means better-matched impressions, less wasted spend, and stronger performance across campaigns. We'll continue rolling out new partnerships throughout the year — reach out if you'd like early access.
