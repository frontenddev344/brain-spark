The loop that works

Rewards Rabbit keeps things simple: spin the wheel, collect coins, and redeem them for real gift cards. That straightforward gameplay loop has made it one of the stickiest apps in the BrainSpark portfolio, with daily check-ins driving consistent engagement.

Fueling the data flywheel

Every spin, redemption, and daily streak feeds first-party data back into BrainSpark's ecosystem — data that powers smarter targeting across Spark DSP and helps advertisers reach genuinely engaged shoppers rather than passive impressions.
