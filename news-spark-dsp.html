Spark DSP Now Live Across More Retail Networks

Spark DSP, our self-serve demand-side platform, now plugs directly into a wider set of retail media networks — giving advertisers a faster path from campaign setup to live impressions, with first-party data powering every placement.

What's new

The latest integrations let advertisers reach shoppers across more owned-and-operated retail channels without juggling separate dashboards or duplicating audience segments. Campaigns built in Spark DSP now sync automatically with each connected network's inventory.

Why it matters

Retail media only works when the data behind it is fresh and first-party. Because Spark DSP is wired directly into BrainSpark's own mobile app portfolio, every impression is informed by real shopper behavior — not third-party guesswork — which means tighter targeting and better performance from day one.

"Every new network we connect means advertisers reach real, engaged shoppers — backed by data we own, not data we rent."

— Spark DSP Product Lead

What's next

We're continuing to expand network coverage throughout the year. If you're running retail media campaigns and want early access to new placements as they roll out, reach out to our team.
